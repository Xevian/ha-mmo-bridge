║         MMO Hub — Setup Guide               ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Welcome! This object connects your Second Life
presence to Home Assistant so the owner can see
when you're online, send you messages in-world,
and run automations based on your status.

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 1 — Register
───────────────────────────────────────────────

Make sure your group tag is active, then TOUCH
this object.

You'll receive an IM confirming registration.
That's it — you're connected!

If you're already registered and touch again,
your HUD URL will be refreshed automatically.

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 2 — Wear the HUD
───────────────────────────────────────────────

You should have received an MMO HUD object in
your inventory. Wear it as an attachment.

After wearing it, touch this Hub object once —
the Hub will send your Home Assistant URL to
the HUD automatically. You only need to do this
once (or after a HUD reset).

See the "MMO HUD - Setup Guide" notecard inside
your HUD for HUD-specific instructions.

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  WHAT GETS TRACKED
───────────────────────────────────────────────

  • Online / offline status
  • Whether you're at the home parcel
  • Region and parcel name (via HUD)
  • AFK and busy flags (via HUD)

Your position is sent but only used for the
device tracker — it's not stored or logged.

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  RECEIVING MESSAGES
───────────────────────────────────────────────

The owner can send you IMs from Home Assistant
automations. They'll arrive as normal IMs from
this object and look like:

  ====================
  == Dinner's ready!
  ====================

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  HA SCRIPTS (the fun bit!)
───────────────────────────────────────────────

The owner can set up Home Assistant scripts that
you can trigger from your HUD — things like
toggling lights, activating scenes, or any other
HA automation.

Just ask the owner to label a HA script with
"MMO Script" and it will appear in your HUD
touch menu automatically.

Want something private just for you? Ask the
owner to label a script "MMO - Your Name" and
only your HUD will see it.

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  NEED TO UNREGISTER?
───────────────────────────────────────────────

Ask the owner to run:  /5 remove Your Name

───────────────────────────────────────────────
  PROBLEMS?
───────────────────────────────────────────────

Not receiving IMs? Make sure you registered by
touching the Hub with your group tag active.

HUD not connecting? Touch the Hub while wearing
the HUD to push the URL to it again.

Contact the owner if you continue to have
trouble.
